#### 183. Number 373₁₀ is equivalent in the binary system to:

1. $$101110101$$
2. $$101010101$$
3. $$100110101$$
4. $$101110011$$

<details>

<summary>Show me the answer</summary>

**Answer:** 1. $$101110101$$

**Explanation:**

* Converting decimal 373 to binary:
  * 373 ÷ 2 = 186 remainder 1
  * 186 ÷ 2 = 93 remainder 0
  * 93 ÷ 2 = 46 remainder 1
  * 46 ÷ 2 = 23 remainder 0
  * 23 ÷ 2 = 11 remainder 1
  * 11 ÷ 2 = 5 remainder 1
  * 5 ÷ 2 = 2 remainder 1
  * 2 ÷ 2 = 1 remainder 0
  * 1 ÷ 2 = 0 remainder 1
  * Thus, 373 in decimal is 101110101 in binary.

</details>
