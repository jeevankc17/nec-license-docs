### 9. If a delta connection has 3 ohms resistance, what is the equivalent resistance in star connection?

1. 1 ohm
2. 3 ohms
3. 9 ohms
4. 27 ohms

<details>

<summary>Show me the answer</summary>

#### Answer:

1. 1 ohm

#### Explanation:

The equivalent resistance in a star connection is given by dividing the delta resistance by 3. Therefore, $$( R_{star} = \frac{R_{delta}}{3} = \frac{3}{3} = 1 \text{ ohm} )$$.

</details>
